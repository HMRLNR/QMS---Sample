QMS Page Help

Upload an Ebook

This section is for users who are interested in submitting ebooks for publication on our site. Here's how it works:

  1. Prepare your ebook file in a compatible format (e.g., EPUB, PDF).
  2. Click the "Upload an Ebook" button on the submission page.
  3. Fill out the necessary information about your ebook (title, description, etc.).
  4. Submit your ebook for review.

Once you submit your ebook, our Quality Assurance Staff (QMS) will review it and notify you via email about the approval status.

User Notification

After submitting your ebook, you'll receive an email notification from the QMS regarding the approval status. The email will contain:

  • The subject line will indicate whether your ebook is approved or not.
  • The email body will provide details on the decision and any further instructions.

We recommend checking your spam folder if you don't see the notification email in your inbox within a reasonable timeframe.

Admin Login (QMS)

This section is for authorized Quality Assurance Staff members only. Use the login form provided on the separate admin panel to access the submitted ebooks for review.

Once logged in, you'll be able to:

  • Review submitted ebooks.
  • Approve or reject ebooks based on our quality guidelines.
  • Send notification emails to users regarding their ebook status.

**Please note:** Admin login credentials are for authorized QMS staff only.
